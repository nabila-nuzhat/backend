(TCP vs UDP)
    - TCP (Transmission Control Protocol) and UDP (User Datagram Protocol) are the two primary methods for sending data across the internet. TCP is reliable, connection-oriented, and verifies that all data arrives perfectly (ideal for web browsing). UDP is connectionless and faster, but skips error-checking (ideal for gaming and live streaming)
